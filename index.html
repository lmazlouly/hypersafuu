Tokenomics

3% Marketing
3% of all transactions will go to our team marketing wallet to ensure we are able to come up with the best campaigns to get a great return on your investment
2% Buy back
2% of every transaction is transferred into liquidity Pool for Pancakeswap to create a stable price floor.
HIGHEST BUSD Rewards
Automatically earn 8% Binance pegged BUSD rewards just for holding HyperSafuu . 8% of every buy/sell is awarded to holders automatically in their wallets.
1% Auto Liquidity
1% of every buy/sell is automatically deposited into the locked liquidity wallet as $BNB, providing continuous liquidity to aid in price stabilization. The liquidity pool is locked and certified.
